Key Features

Vehicle & Community Sharing

Easily share your own vehicles or access shared vehicles within your network. Reduce idle time, maximize fleet utilization, and build a collaborative logistics ecosystem.

Load Management

Organize, assign, and track cargo loads across your fleet. Ensure timely deliveries with structured load creation and distribution tools.

Role-Based Access Control

Support for two main roles: Dispatchers can manage fleet activity, and vehicle and load management. Drivers can focus on focused access for safe and efficient operations.

Task Assignment & Tracking

Dispatchers can assign loads, set schedules, manage trips, and track real-time status updates for complete operational visibility.

Secure & Organized Operations

Driver access is streamlined to reduce errors and maximize focus, while all data is securely managed to protect your operations.

Collaborative Fleet Use

Built for communities and teams who want to share resources, follow vehicle activity, and reduce operational costs through intelligent asset sharing.

Web App Features, Dispatcher

Dispatchers get access to a comprehensive control center, including:

  • My Vehicles: Manage owned and shared vehicles, and track destinations.
  • Community Sharing: View and interact with shared vehicles.
  • Load Management: Create, publish, and track loads.
  • Requests & Approvals: View incoming usage requests from other companies.
  • Member Directory: Manage connected companies, including blocking/unblocking.
  • Insights: Monitor followed vehicles and shared resources for performance optimization.

Mobile App Features, Driver Experience

Drivers have a dedicated mobile experience that includes:

  • Vehicle Selection: Choose from assigned or shared vehicles.
  • Live Location Tracking: View real-time location on the map.
  • Status Updates: Easily update delivery or trip status (Available, Loading, etc.)
  • Cargo Capacity Trend: Specify remaining available space in kg.
  • Destination Planning: Add or update delivery destinations on the go.
  • Vehicle Switching: Seamlessly switch to another assigned vehicle if needed.
